Questions and answers about IAGeyser-rewrite:

Q: Why did I choose to make a rewrite instead of a plugin?
A: I looked at current paid ones, such as furnace, and they are external, which allows them to use any language and extension(s)
Q: Why Open-Source?
A: I want everyone to see what is used to convert the packs, modify it, etc. Also geyser is Open-Source too!
Q: What does it do right now?
A: Im working on items, with blocks coming soon.
Q: How will model conversion work?
A: I dont know yet...
Q: Does it convert all of the blocks for you?
A: Yes and no, it is an automation tool to streamline the process, outputs may require editing, you will need to tell it the custom model data information and path(s)
